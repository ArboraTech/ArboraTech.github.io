Phase 3

Methane Capture

Summary

In this final phase, we plan to upgrade our technology to capture and process other greenhouse gases as well, mainly methane. Methane, though found in lower levels in the atmosphere, is a much more potent greenhouse gas because of its increased heat capture capacity. As such, in this final phase, we plan to expand our technology to incorporate all the carbon-based greenhouse gases that have a major impact on climate change, and process them into usable products.

We also plan on experimenting with the production of energy with the captured CO2, and develop more portable carbon capture and process devices.

‍

What We Plan To Do

1. Improve Carbon Capture Plants

2. Open More Carbon Capture Plants

3. Build Plants to Capture All Carbon-Based Greenhouse Gases

4. Develop Processes to Utilize Other Greenhouse Gases for Production

5. Partner With Factories and Oil Companies To Install Carbon Capture and Processing Devices In Factories

6. Stop Climate Change!

‍

‍

We've got work to do

We still have a long way to go before we can safely say we are stopping climate change. First, we need to find ways to remove large amounts of carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gases to have a large enough impact on slowing the progression of climate change. We also need to accelerate the research on using more carbon-based greenhouse gases to produce innovative technology like graphene and carbon nanotubes.

Lastly, a lot of work needs to be done to make the processes simpler and more efficient to allow distribution to factories, allowing us to tap the problem at the source with large impact.
